CPA Advisory Services: How CPAs Are Pivoting from Compliance to Consulting

📅 March 6, 2026 ⏱️ 10 min read 📊 720/mo searches

The CPA profession is at an inflection point. With 720 monthly searches for "CPA advisory" and growing, the market is signaling what we already know: compliance alone is no longer enough.

The Advisory Imperative

Key stats driving the shift:

  • AICPA reports advisory revenue now accounts for 30%+ of firm revenue at top-performing firms
  • Advisory services grow at 15-20% annually vs. 3-5% for compliance
  • Firms with strong advisory practices have 2x higher margins
  • Client retention is 40% higher for firms offering advisory

Building Your CPA Advisory Practice

1. Start with Your Best Clients

You already have clients who need advisory help. Start with 3-5 who are growing, making big decisions, or asking financial questions that go beyond bookkeeping.

2. Package Your Knowledge

Create 2-3 advisory packages at different price points. Make it easy for clients to say yes.

3. Change the Conversation

Stop leading with "we do your taxes and books." Lead with "we help you make better financial decisions." The positioning shift changes everything about how clients perceive your value.

4. Invest in Technology

Advisory requires better reporting tools. Invest in Fathom, Reach Reporting, or Jirav to deliver polished, professional advisory deliverables.

5. Hire for Advisory

As advisory revenue grows, hire analysts and associates who can do the analytical heavy-lifting while you focus on client strategy and relationships.
