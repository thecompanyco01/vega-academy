Building a KPI Dashboard for Clients

A well-designed KPI dashboard is the single most powerful advisory deliverable. Here's how to build one:

  1. Select 8-12 KPIs relevant to the client's industry and stage
  2. Set benchmarks using industry data and historical trends
  3. Automate data feeds from QBO/Xero where possible
  4. Design for clarity — traffic light colors, trend arrows, sparklines
  5. Present monthly with narrative commentary and action items

From KPI Tracking to Advisory Revenue

Here's the key insight: KPI dashboards are the entry drug to advisory services.

Start by adding a simple KPI report to your monthly bookkeeping deliverable. When clients start asking "why is our margin declining?" or "how do we improve cash flow?" — that's when you upsell to full advisory.
