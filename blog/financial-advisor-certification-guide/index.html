For Bookkeepers Going Advisory: CMA Wins

While CFP and CFA are prestigious, the CMA is the most relevant certification for bookkeepers transitioning to business advisory because:

  • It focuses on business financial management, not personal financial planning
  • Covers the exact skills advisory clients need: budgeting, forecasting, performance analysis
  • Faster and more affordable than CFP or CFA
  • Growing demand as companies need internal financial strategy, not just external compliance

The Reality Check

You don't need ANY certification to start offering advisory services. The most successful fractional CFOs we know built their practices on experience and results, not alphabet soup after their names.

Certifications help with credibility and knowledge — but they're not a prerequisite. Start offering advisory services now, get certified in parallel.
