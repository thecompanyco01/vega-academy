No. Fractional CFO work focuses on strategic financial guidance — cash flow management, KPI tracking, forecasting, and business advice. Your bookkeeping experience is your biggest asset. Many successful fractional CFOs started as bookkeepers without a CPA.
How quickly can I start earning advisory revenue?
Most students are ready to pitch their first advisory package within 2-4 weeks. The Foundations Course walks you through exactly how to package, price, and sell advisory services — including scripts for the actual conversation with clients.
